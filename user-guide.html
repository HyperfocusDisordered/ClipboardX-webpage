ClipboardX User Guide

Instant Preview

Shortcut: Tab

While browsing clipboard items, hold Tab to preview the selected content instantly or zoom into elements for detailed inspection. This helps you quickly verify content before using it.

Show ClipboardX

Shortcut: ⌥V (Option + V)

Press ⌥V to open ClipboardX under your cursor. Access your clipboard history without disrupting workflow.

Flash Paste with ⌘V

Shortcut: ⌘V (Command + V) with additional navigation while holding ⌘

Transforms ⌘V into an interactive paste—similar to ⌘Tab for app switching—while retaining regular paste functionality.

  1. Initiate Flash Paste: Press ⌘V and keep holding ⌘.
  2. Navigate Items:
    • While holding ⌘, press V repeatedly to cycle through items,
      • or
    • Press a number key (e.g., 3) while holding ⌘ to select by position,
      • or
    • Move your cursor over the desired item while holding ⌘.
  3. Paste Selected Item: Release ⌘ to paste the highlighted item.

AI Text Recognition for Images

Global Shortcut: ⌥T (Option + T)

Instantly paste last copied item as AI-recognized text. Perfect for getting text from screenshots, photos, or any other images without manual retyping.

Smart Snapshot Area

Shortcut: ⌥S (Option + S)

Hold ⌥S and move your mouse to select an area. Release to capture and save the snapshot to your clipboard.

Snapshot and Paste as Text

Shortcut: ⌥S + ⌥T (Smart Snapshot Area + AI Text Recognition)

Smart Snapshot Area and AI Text Recognition work perfectly together. Capture any area of your screen and instantly extract text from it.

  1. Capture Area: Hold ⌥S and select the area you want to capture.
  2. Paste as Text: Press ⌥T to instantly paste the captured image as AI-recognized text.

Alternative with Flash Paste: Use ⌘V V T + release to select any image from your clipboard history and extract text from it.

Grab&Drop

Shortcut: ⌥D (Option + D)

ClipboardX autocopies your drags too. So you can start dragging any item and just release the mouse to grab it into ClipboardX. Then you can press Option-D anywhere to instantly drop taken item under your cursor.

  1. Start Dragging: Start dragging any file, text, or content from anywhere on your Mac.
  2. Release Mouse Button: At this point ClipboardX has already picked up your drag, just release the mouse button.
  3. Drop Anywhere: Press ⌥D anywhere to instantly drop the grabbed item under your cursor.

ClipboardX completely unifies Copy&Paste and Drag&Drop experiences into one seamless workflow. You no longer need separate apps like Drop Zone or other clipboard managers - ClipboardX does this much faster and more conveniently.

Smart Screen Record

Shortcut: ⌥R (Option + R)

  1. Start Recording: Hold ⌥R, (move your mouse to select an area if needed), then release to begin recording.
  2. Stop Recording: Press ⌥R again to finish and save.

Start and stop screen recordings effortlessly. Move your mouse to select the desired recording area while holding ⌥R.

Better Multiple Selection

Shortcut: Shift + Cursor Movement

Hold Shift and move your cursor over items to select multiple clipboard entries for batch actions.

Smart Color Shot

Shortcut: ⌥C (Option + C)

Hold ⌥C and move your mouse to pick the color under the cursor. Release to copy the color code to your clipboard.
