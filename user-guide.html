ClipboardX User Guide

Instant Preview

Shortcut: Tab

While browsing clipboard items, hold Tab to preview the selected content instantly. This helps you quickly verify content before using it.

Show ClipboardX

Shortcut: ⌥V (Option + V)

Press ⌥V to open ClipboardX under your cursor. Access your clipboard history without disrupting workflow.

Flash Paste with ⌘V

Shortcut: ⌘V (Command + V) with additional navigation while holding ⌘

  1. Initiate Flash Paste: Press ⌘V and keep holding ⌘.
  2. Navigate Items:
    • While holding ⌘, press V repeatedly to cycle through items,
      • or
    • Press a number key (e.g., 3) while holding ⌘ to select by position,
      • or
    • Move your cursor over the desired item while holding ⌘.
  3. Paste Selected Item: Release ⌘ to paste the highlighted item.

Transforms ⌘V into an interactive paste—similar to ⌘Tab for app switching—while retaining regular paste functionality.

AI Text Recognition for Images

Global Shortcut: ⌥T (Option + T)

Instantly paste last copied item as AI-recognized text. Perfect for getting text from screenshots, photos, or any other images without manual retyping.

Advanced Usage with Flash Paste

Shortcut: ⌘T (Command + T) while in Flash Paste mode

  1. Quick Selection Method:
    • Press ⌘V and hold ⌘
    • Press V repeatedly to select desired image
    • Press T while still holding ⌘
    • Release ⌘ to paste recognized text from the selected image
  2. Example: ⌘V V T + release will extract and paste text from the second image in your clipboard history

Smart Snapshot Area

Shortcut: ⌥S (Option + S)

Hold ⌥S and drag to select an area. Release to capture and save the snapshot to your clipboard.

Closing ClipboardX

Shortcut: Esc

Press Esc at any time to close ClipboardX, previews, or preferences, returning you to work. This is the default action for closing dialogs in macOS.

Smart Screen Record

Shortcut: ⌥R (Option + R)

  1. Start Recording: Hold ⌥R and release to begin recording.
  2. Stop Recording: Press ⌥R again to finish and save.

Start and stop screen recordings effortlessly.

Better Multiple Selection

Shortcut: Shift + Cursor Movement

Hold Shift and move your cursor over items to select multiple clipboard entries for batch actions.

Drop Mode

Shortcut: ⌥D (Option + D)

  1. Activate Drop Mode: Press ⌥D to open Drop Mode under your cursor.
  2. Drop Content: Click an item to insert its content instantly at your cursor's location.

Quickly drop content with a single click.
