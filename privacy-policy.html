ClipboardX Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 6 March 2024

Owner and Operator of ClipboardX

The desktop application ClipboardX and the related website clipboardx.app (the “Website”) are owned and operated by ClipboardX Team (“we”, “us”, and “our”).

Screen Recording and Screenshot Features

ClipboardX provides users with an easier way to create screen recordings and take screenshots for their personal use. These recordings and screenshots are saved locally on your Mac in the ~/Library/ScreenRecordings folder or copied to your clipboard. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any data from your screen recordings or screenshots. All content remains solely on your device unless you choose to share it.

Data Storage

ClipboardX stores your data (clipboard history, pinboard items, etc.) locally on your Mac. This data is not transferred to or stored on our servers, nor do we have access to, own, or use this data in any other manner.

Permissions

ClipboardX requires certain permissions to function correctly, such as access to your clipboard, screen recording capabilities, and screenshot functionalities. These permissions are used solely to provide the intended features of the app. We do not collect or transmit any personal data through these permissions.

Crash Reporting

If ClipboardX crashes, we collect anonymized data to help us identify and fix the issue in a future update. This data includes information about the app version, operating system, and device you’re using but does not include any personal data or content from your clipboard, screen recordings, or screenshots.

We use Firebase for crash reporting.

Usage Analytics

We collect anonymized information about how you use ClipboardX to help us improve the application. This data cannot identify you personally and includes:

  • Device type and model;
  • Operating system version;
  • Language and region settings;
  • Application version;
  • Events performed within the app (e.g., “user accessed screen recording feature”);
  • Error codes and messages.

We use analytics services like Firebase to collect, store, and analyze this data. Additionally, we measure and analyze usage on our website using Plausible.

Payments

When you make payments related to ClipboardX, the payments are processed by third parties such as Apple or other payment processors. We do not collect your payment data and do not have access to it. Please refer to the privacy policies of the respective payment processors for more details.

Cookies

We do not use any cookies on the Website or ClipboardX.

Support Interactions

When you contact us for support, we collect your email address, name, and any other information you provide in your message to assist you. This data is collected solely to resolve your inquiry. If you’d like your communications removed, please let us know.

We use Help Scout for customer support and Gmail for personal communication.

Marketing Messages

If you subscribe to our newsletter or provide consent for commercial communication, we may send you messages about new features or special offers. You can opt-out at any time by clicking the “unsubscribe” link in the email. Additionally, you may receive important technical notices, which are not considered commercial messages and cannot be opted out from.

We use SendGrid for sending newsletters and transactional emails.

Managing Your Data

Since we do not collect personal data through the app's functionalities, there is no personal data stored by us that you need to manage. If you have interacted with us via support or subscribed to our newsletter, you have rights over your personal data in those contexts, including:

  • Right of access: Obtain a copy of your personal data;
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccuracies in your data;
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data;
  • Right to restriction: Restrict processing of your personal data;
  • Right to object: Object to data processing;
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw your consent to data processing.

If you’d like to exercise your rights, please contact us at info@clipboardx.app. We may require identifying information to verify your request.

Disclosure of Your Personal Data

We do not disclose your personal data to third parties except as necessary for customer support or our legitimate business interests. Data processors that may have access to your personal data include service providers like Help Scout and SendGrid. We ensure that all data processors provide adequate protection for your data.

Protection of Your Data

We use appropriate security measures to protect any personal data you may provide through support interactions or newsletter subscriptions, including secure networks, strong passwords, and restricted access.

Storage Period

We store your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, or until you request its deletion. Afterward, we securely delete the data.

Updates and Questions

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date mentioned above. We may update this policy from time to time. For any questions, please contact us at info@clipboardx.app.
