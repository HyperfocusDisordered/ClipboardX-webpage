ClipboardX Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 6 March 2024

Owner and Operator of ClipboardX

The desktop and mobile applications ClipboardX (collectively, “ClipboardX”) and the related website clipboardx.app (the “Website”) are owned and operated by ClipboardX Team (“we”, “us”, and “our”).

When we ask you to provide us with your personal data or if you contact us, we act as a data controller regarding your personal data. This means that we are responsible for deciding what personal data can be collected from you and how it can be used.

Data Storage

ClipboardX stores your data (clipboard history, pinboard items, etc.) locally on your Mac or iOS device. If you use iCloud Sync, ClipboardX stores a copy of your data in your personal iCloud storage to synchronize it across your devices automatically. You can disable iCloud Sync in ClipboardX’s preferences.

This data is not transferred to or stored on our servers, nor do we have access to, own, or use this data in any other manner.

ClipboardX also stores your subscription information in your iCloud storage to allow you to use it on all your devices. You always have the option to disable ClipboardX’s access to iCloud in your device’s preferences.

Usage Analytics

We collect anonymized information about how you use ClipboardX to help us improve the application. This data cannot identify you personally and includes:

  • Anonymized user ID;
  • Device type, model, and identifier;
  • Operating system;
  • Language;
  • Country, region, city;
  • Application version;
  • Subscription status and duration;
  • Events performed within the app (e.g., “user completed onboarding”);
  • Error codes and messages.

We use analytics services like Mixpanel, Firebase, and ASATools to collect, store, and analyze this data. Additionally, we measure and analyze usage on our website using Plausible.

Crash Reporting

If ClipboardX crashes, we collect anonymized data to help us identify and fix the issue in a future update. This data includes information about the app, operating system, and device you’re using but does not include your personal data.

We use Firebase for crash reporting.

Payments

When you make payments related to ClipboardX, the payments are processed by third parties such as Apple or other payment processors. We do not collect your payment data and do not have access to it. Please refer to the privacy policies of the respective payment processors for more details.

Cookies

We do not use any cookies on the Website or ClipboardX.

Support Interactions

When you contact us for support, we collect your email address, name, and any other information you provide in your message to assist you. This data is collected to resolve your inquiry. If you’d like your communications removed, please let us know.

We use Help Scout for customer support and Gmail for personal communication.

Marketing Messages

If you subscribe to our newsletter, provide consent for commercial communication, or enter into a contract with us, we may send you messages about new features or special offers. You can opt-out at any time by clicking the “unsubscribe” link in the email. Additionally, you may receive important technical notices, which are not considered commercial messages and cannot be opted out from.

We use SendGrid for sending newsletters and transactional emails.

Managing Your Data

You have rights over your personal data, including:

  • Right of access: Obtain a copy of your personal data;
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccuracies in your data;
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data;
  • Right to restriction: Restrict processing of your personal data;
  • Right to portability: Request a copy of your data in a portable format;
  • Right to object: Object to data processing;
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw your consent to data processing.

If you’d like to exercise your rights, please contact us at info@clipboardx.app. We may require identifying information to verify your request.

Disclosure of Your Personal Data

We do not disclose your personal data to third parties except as necessary for the operation of ClipboardX or our legitimate business interests. Data processors that may have access to your personal data include service providers like Mixpanel, Firebase, and ASATools. We ensure that all data processors provide adequate protection for your data.

Protection of Your Data

We use appropriate security measures to protect your personal data, including secure networks, strong passwords, and restricted access. Personal data is anonymized where possible.

Storage Period

We store your personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected, or until you request its deletion. Afterward, we securely delete the data.

Non-personal data may be retained for business purposes. Legal requirements may also necessitate data retention for specific periods.

Updates and Questions

This Privacy Policy is effective as of the date mentioned above. We may update this policy from time to time. For any questions, please contact us at info@clipboardx.app.
